About Me

My name is Sophia McLeod, and I am a Sophomore here at Miami University. I am an ETBD major with a minor in Healthcare Sales. Post-Grad, I would like to work at a Medical Device Company, and sell product. I have always been passionate about the medical world, so I would like to remain in that field. I grew up in a small suburban town in Southwest Michigan called, Kalamazoo. Which is known for its eclectic breweries, and the large corporations it houses. Here at Miami, I am apart of the Sorority, Gamma Phi Beta, in which I have met some of my life long friends. My favorite part about being a student at Miami is the sense of community. Its heartwarming to know that most people here share this love for the school, and for Oxford, Ohio. When I am at home in Michigan and meet an Alumni, we are able to talk for hours about how much we love Miami.
